About Us

Ayah’s Henna Haven is a boutique henna service in Richmond, VA that creates beautiful, intricate designs for every occasion. Specializing in traditional and modern styles, Ayah crafts each design to match your vision. Using only natural, high-quality henna, Ayah’s Henna Haven ensures stunning, safe results for all skin types.

With over 4 years of experience in the art of henna, Ayah has perfected her craft and has had the privilege of working with a variety of clients, from brides to festival-goers. Each design is a reflection of the individual's personality and occasion.
